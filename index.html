Our Practice Areas

Asylum

Asylum is a legal protection that the United States grants to people who have suffered persecution or fear persecution in their country of origin based on race, religion, nationality, political opinion, or membership in a particular social group. This protection allows the person to live and work legally in the country and, over time, apply for permanent residence.

Special Immigrant Juvenile Status (SIJS)

The Juvenile Visa, known as Special Immigrant Juvenile Status (SIJS), is an immigration option for minors under 21 years of age who have been abandoned, abused or neglected by one or both parents. This status allows certain young immigrants to obtain legal permanent residence (Green Card) in the United States.
